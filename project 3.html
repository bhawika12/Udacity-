Penguin



A penguin's striking coloring is a matter of camouflage; from above, its black back blends into the murky depths of the ocean.

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscidae
  • Average Length: 43cm
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Habitat: Southern Hemisphere
All penguins have a white belly and a black back. Penguins cannot fly, but they can swim very well. They have good hearing. The white and black colors are for camouflage when they swim. So, when a predator looking from underwater sees the white belly and wings of the penguin, they can not see it well with the light coming from above.
